About

I am Eemil Voitk from Norway, a pianist for twelve years and soon-to-be organist. I enjoy creating across mediums, from writing and art to digital projects such as Minecraft maps and my current Rain World region. I am drawn to unsettling work — such as the paintings of Zdzisław Beksiński, Vilhelm Hammershøi, and Soviet animations. This site is a space for projects, experiments, and things I care about, updated whenever something is ready to share.
